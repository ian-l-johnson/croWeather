<< croWeather >>

Desktop current weather condition display. (epaper /w rPi /w housing)

- get weather conditions either online or local station
- display weather on epaper (crow themed; sunshine, umbrella, snow, etc.)
- housing made from steel or rock or ...

Display:
-Temp C/F
-Mode (see below)
-Picture(s) (crow,sun,moon,etc)

Modes:
-Day_sun
-Day_suncloud
-Day_cloud
-Day_rain
-Day_snow
-Night_clear
-Night_cloud
-Night_partcloud
-Night_rain
-Night_snow
